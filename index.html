About Me

​Hello! I'm Chaoyi Luo.​​ I hold a Master's degree in Electronic Information from Shanghai Film Academy, Shanghai University. I have a passion for physics-based character animation, reinforcement learning, and robotics. My final goal is to make virtual characters and real robots more human-like, emotional, and interactive.

Currently, I am focusing on developing techniques for procedurally generating animations of musical instrument performances. Additionally, I am exploring how these techniques can be applied to robotics.[Google Scholar]
