About Me

Hello! I'm Chaoyi Luo. I am currently a third-year master's student at Shanghai Film Academy, where I am pursuing a master degree in computer science.

My research interests lie in computer animation, deep reinforcement learning, and robotics. Currently, I am focusing on developing techniques for procedurally generating animations of musical instrument performances. Additionally, I am exploring how these techniques can be applied to robotics, especially in sim-to-real scenarios.
